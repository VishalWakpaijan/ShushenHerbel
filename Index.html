    "Sushen"

    We derive our name from the royal Vaidhya Shushen from Ramayana. He's popularly known for his greatest gift to mankind. "Sanjeevani Booti" a life-elixir herb. It was Vaidhya Shushen who guided Hanuman Ji to bring Sanjeevani Booti from the Himalayas

    Our Story

    “For every illness or ailment known to mankind, God has created a plant that will heal it. We just need to keep discovering the properties for natural healing.” Dr Anil Agrawal

    Shushen Herbals is the brainchild of Dr Anil Agrawal, who established the brand in 1994 at Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh. He cured many Jain priests at the very beginning of his career. Their pure and simple lifestyle further promoted the importance of pure, grounded ingredients in Ayurveda. Hence, the quality of ingredients is the secret to our success. We have cultivated authentic, unadulterated sources of ingredients over the years, and we continue to cultivate them in an increasingly polluted world. Dr Anil Agrawal personally supervises the production process of all syrups, kadhas, capsules, etc. Shushen Herbal is an Ayurvedic Company Approved by the WHO-GMP and Ayush Ministry. With an experience of more than 25 years and more than 2 lakh loyal customers nationwide, we are one of the pioneers in the modern-day Ayurveda industry in India.

    Our Team

    Dr. Anil Agrawal

    Dr Anil Agrawal is the raison-d'etre behind Sushen Herbals. His knowledge about the medicinal properties of plants and the years he has spent healing people are at the centre of our offerings. He takes great pride in the rigorous standards he holds for the raw materials that go into each product on the shelves. The decades of experience he holds makes him the brand of the company.

    Shivam Rawat

    Shivam Rawat is a young scientist and a serial entrepreneur. He completed his masters from ICT Mumbai, one of India's deemed university for research. He has a proven academic and entrepreneurial track record. Hailing from a family of farmers, Shivam was fascinated by the medicinal properties of plants. At 7 years of age, he grew his first Ashwagandha plant on a piece of infertile land. Shivam is an integral part of Shushen Herbal family. His keen interest in Ayurveda and his knack for research has helped Shushen produce high quality authentic and natural products.
